## 4. ROI CALCULATOR FRAMEWORK

### 4.1 Input Variables

**Current State Metrics**:

1. Number of RFPs responded to per month
2. Average hours per RFP (industry average: 40-80 hours)
3. Average hourly cost of proposal team (typically $75-$150/hour)
4. Current win rate (industry average: 10-20%)
5. Average contract value
6. Number of proposal team members

**Target State Metrics** (with RFP Agent):

1. Hours per RFP reduced by 60-70%
2. Win rate improved by 15-25%
3. Capacity to bid on 2-3x more opportunities
4. Quality score improvement (compliance, readability)

### 4.2 ROI Calculation Formulas

**Time Savings Value**:

```
Monthly Time Saved = (RFPs/month × Hours/RFP × Automation %)
Monthly Labor Cost Saved = Monthly Time Saved × Hourly Rate
Annual Labor Savings = Monthly Labor Cost Saved × 12
```

**Revenue Impact**:

```
Current Annual Wins = (RFPs/month × 12) × Current Win Rate
New Annual Wins = (RFPs/month × 12 × Capacity Multiplier) × New Win Rate
Additional Wins = New Annual Wins - Current Annual Wins
Additional Revenue = Additional Wins × Average Contract Value
```

**Total ROI**:

```
Total Annual Value = Annual Labor Savings + Additional Revenue
Annual Subscription Cost = Monthly Cost × 12
Net Annual Benefit = Total Annual Value - Annual Subscription Cost
ROI % = (Net Annual Benefit / Annual Subscription Cost) × 100
Payback Period = Annual Subscription Cost / (Monthly Labor Cost Saved + Monthly Revenue Impact)
```

## 6. PRICING PSYCHOLOGY & TACTICS

### 6.1 Anchoring Strategy

**High Anchor**:

- Lead with Enterprise tier ($2,500/user/month)
- Position as "what large contractors pay"
- Makes Professional tier ($299) seem like incredible value

**Comparison Anchor**:

- "RFPIO charges $1,500/user. We're $299 with 3x more features."
- "Replace 5 tools with one platform at 1/10th the cost"

**Value Anchor**:

- "Save $40,000/month in labor costs for $3,000/month"
- "Win one more $200K contract to pay for 5 years of service"

### 6.2 Decoy Pricing

**Professional** ($299) vs **Business** ($599):

- Business tier is intentionally 2x Professional
- Most customers will choose Business (higher value perception)
- Creates "good, better, best" perception
- Professional serves as price anchor

### 6.3 Upsell Triggers

**Automated Upgrade Prompts**:

1. **Portal Limit Reached**: "You've connected 10 portals. Upgrade to Business for unlimited portals."
2. **User Limit**: "Add more team members with Business tier (up to 50 users)."
3. **Automation Need**: "Enable browser automation by upgrading to Business."
4. **Volume Scaling**: "Processing 50+ RFPs/month? Business tier optimizes for high volume."

**Success-Based Upsells**:

1. **High Win Rate**: "You're winning 25% of bids! Enterprise tier can scale your success."
2. **Time Savings**: "You've saved 1,000 hours! Unlock more automation with Business tier."
3. **Revenue Growth**: "You've won $2M in contracts! Invest in Enterprise for 10x scale."

### 6.4 Discount Strategy

**Strategic Discounts** (maintain margin):

- **Annual Prepay**: 15% discount (improves cash flow)
- **Multi-Year**: Additional 10% (locks in customers)
- **Volume**: 10% for 20+ users, 20% for 50+ users
- **Early Adopter**: 50% off first 3 months (beta customers)
- **Referral**: $500 credit per referred customer

**Prohibited Discounts**:

- ❌ Never discount below 30% (devalues product)
- ❌ No discounts without commitment (annual minimum)
- ❌ No discounts for late-stage enterprise (they have budget)
